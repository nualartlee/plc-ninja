Sawmills

Providing extensive services to the lumber industry, from troubleshooting to the full design and commissioning of new systems such as debarkers, canters, sorters, planers, chippers, banders, etc...

Lumber mills very often employ Rockwell Automation® PLC and HMI hardware, ControlLogix® being the favourite platform. I will confidently provide programming/commissioning services for your projects on this or any other platform, as well as education and training for personnel.

In supporting my clients, I often provide assistance troubleshooting erratic behaviour in systems such as sorters, saw movement timing, communication between systems, and configuration of new equipment. No problem is too big or too small.

Research & Design

My favourite line of work, developing R&D projects allows me the satisfaction of creating entirely new systems that my clients love.

Some R&D projects I have pursued include automated drill rig control to provide high accuracy directional drilling, self contained gold concentrators that offer fully automated control without a PLC (all controls implemented in a VFD), tree planters, energy systems and more.

The picture above displays a model pump jack I built to develop VFD functionality that would monitor the health of the pump system. The status of a well and health of a pump jack can be accurately determined according to the torque being applied throughout the pumping cycle.

HMI & SCADA

With the IoT age upon us, scada and HMI systems are being rapidly transformed. Stakeholders benefit greatly having all the data available anywhere at any time, and industrial control and supervision is inevitably moving towards the internet and cloud based systems.

Whether replacing legacy HMI's or commissioning the latest cloud services, I look forward to helping you with your scada platform selection and implementation.

IT

With industrial control moving ever closer to software based solutions, knowledge of modern technologies is essential for industry. Technologies such as virtualization and cloud services are becoming an essential part of automation systems. Let me help you implement and manage these modern systems.

This new broad connectivity and complexity in automation also brings new security concerns, with disruptive events to industrial processes ever increasing. When working together, I will be happy to provide guidance to minimize threats.

Just like R&D, software development provides a deep satisfaction when I create a product that greatly benefits my client. Let me know what you envision and we can work to make it a reality.

I love Python and I am competent in C#, although I will be happy to adapt to any language you require. Examples of products I have developed include graphic label design software in use by a large lumber manufacturer and API's to integrate scanners and printers to automation systems.
